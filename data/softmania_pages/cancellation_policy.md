# Cancellation & Refund Policy
At SoftMania, we are committed to delivering high-quality online courses and training programs designed to transform careers through real-time, practical learning.
Please note the following regarding cancellations and refunds. For additional contact options, visit </contact-us>.
## No Cancellations
Once a course, program, or training service is purchased, cancellations are not permitted under any circumstances.
## No Refunds
All payments made to SoftMania are final and non-refundable. We do not offer refunds or credits for partial use, non-attendance, or withdrawal from a course or training program.
## Transferability
Enrollments are non-transferable. Purchased courses or training sessions cannot be transferred or deferred.
## Exceptions
In rare situations where SoftMania is unable to deliver a course due to unforeseen circumstances (such as trainer unavailability or technical issues from our side), we will provide alternative arrangements, such as rescheduling or course credit. Monetary refunds will not be issued.
By completing your purchase, you acknowledge and agree to this Cancellation & Refund Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at [support@grad2it.in](mailto:support@grad2it.in) or </contact-us>.
