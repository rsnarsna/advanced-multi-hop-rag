# Refund Policy
## Returns
Unfortunately, we are not offering a refund or exchange.
### Additional non-returnable items
* Gift cards
* Downloadable software products & documents
To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.
There are certain situations where only partial refunds are granted (if applicable), such as any item that is returned more than 10 days after delivery.
## Refunds (if applicable)
Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email regarding approval or rejection. If approved, your refund will be processed and applied to your original payment method within 15 to 30 working days.
## Late or Missing Refunds (if applicable)
If you haven’t received a refund, check your bank account again, then contact your credit card company. It may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next, contact your bank. If you still have not received your refund, please contact us at [info@softmania.in](mailto:info@softmania.in).
## Sale items
Only regular-priced items may be refunded; sale items cannot be refunded.
## Exchanges
Not applicable.
## Gifts
Not applicable.
## Shipping
Not applicable.
