## About Soft Mania
Soft Mania is a career transformation company founded by Muruganantham Pothanaickar, a Splunk Trainer and Mentor who has trained 1500+ learners worldwide.
We offer practical, real-time, project-based learning experiences to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and IT industry readiness. Our mission is simple: empower learners with clarity, confidence, and the skills needed to excel in their Splunk careers.
